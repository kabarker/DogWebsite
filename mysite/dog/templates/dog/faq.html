Frequently Asked Questions

How old does my dog have to be to start a class?

As long as they have all of their shots then they are good to start. We do have some classes that are geared towards puppies so if you have an older dog, make sure you don't sign up for those.

What if my dog doesn't know anything?

That's what classes our for! You're in the right place, our obedience classes don't require your dog know anything but our agility classes do.

What if my dog already knows agility commands?

If you're dog already knows agility then you can schedule a meeting with us where we will evaluate where your dog is at and place you in the correct class.

Is there a place to potty my dog?

We have plenty of space outside to walk and potty your dog and recommend you come early to do so as there is a $5 fee if your dog goes potty in the building.
